About Us

Ra technical services is a service provider to the industries with innovative solutions in the field of Boiler Services, Water/ Waste Water/ Cooling Water/ Chilled water treatments and Systems. We import and supply highest quality SS 316L tubes, fittings, valves, diaphragm replacements for hygienic applications. We supply speciality chemicals for water treatment of Boilers, RO Plants, Cooling Water systems and Waste Water treatment systems. We also supply Water and Waste Water treatment plants, Steam engineering products and Calorifiers.
